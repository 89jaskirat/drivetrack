## 2. Why We Collect Your Information (Purposes)

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

1. **Account creation and authentication** — to verify your identity and maintain your account
2. **App personalization** — to show you zone-relevant content, pricing, and community discussions
3. **Expense and mileage tracking** — to store and sync your financial records across devices
4. **Community features** — to enable you to post, comment, and interact in zone-based forums
5. **Security and fraud prevention** — to detect suspicious activity and protect all users
6. **Legal compliance** — to meet obligations under Canadian privacy, tax, and consumer protection law
7. **Service improvement** — aggregated, anonymized usage analytics to improve the App
8. **Communications** — transactional emails (password reset, account notices) and, with your consent, optional App notifications

We will not use your information for any purpose other than those listed above without obtaining your additional consent.

## 3. Legal Basis for Processing

Under PIPEDA, we process your personal information on the basis of:

- **Consent** — for all optional data collection (phone number, location, analytics)
- **Contractual necessity** — for information required to provide the Services you requested (account, zone, expense data)
- **Legal obligation** — where required by Canadian law (audit logs, breach notification)
- **Legitimate interests** — for security, fraud prevention, and basic anonymized analytics, balanced against your privacy rights

## 4. Location Data

Location data is particularly sensitive and we handle it with extra care:

- **When:** Location is requested only during the zone-selection step of onboarding, and optionally when you enable GPS drive session features
- **What we access:** Device GPS coordinates at the moment of the request
- **What we do with it:** Compare your coordinates against zone city centers to suggest the nearest zone (within 100 km). We do not continuously track your location unless you explicitly activate a GPS session feature
- **Storage:** Zone-detection coordinates may be stored in your profile to assist with future zone suggestions
- **Your control:** You may deny the location permission at any time. Your device OS settings allow you to revoke location permission permanently. Denying location permission does not prevent you from using the App — you can select your zone manually

## 7. Data Security

We implement the following security measures to protect your personal information:

- Encryption in transit (TLS/HTTPS for all communications)
- Encryption at rest for database storage
- Row-level security policies restricting data access to the account owner
- Authentication token expiry and refresh controls
- Audit logging of sensitive operations

**No system is perfectly secure.** We cannot guarantee absolute security. In the event of a breach that creates a real risk of significant harm, we will notify you and the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada as required under PIPEDA.
