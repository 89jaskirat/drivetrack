## Our Community

DriveTrack's community forums are a space for gig-economy and rideshare drivers across Canada to share experiences, ask questions, and support one another. We want the community to be helpful, honest, and welcoming to drivers of all backgrounds and experience levels.

These guidelines apply to all content posted in any DriveTrack sub-forum (c/Canada, c/Calgary, c/Edmonton, etc.) — including posts, comments, replies, and any other user-generated content.

Violation of these guidelines may result in content removal, account suspension, or permanent ban. Serious violations may be reported to law enforcement where required by law.

---

## The Ground Rules

### 1. Be Respectful

Treat every member of this community as you would want to be treated.

- No personal attacks, insults, or harassment toward other users
- No hate speech based on race, ethnicity, national origin, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or any other protected characteristic under the *Canadian Human Rights Act*
- Disagreement is fine — attack ideas, not people
- Do not follow or target another user across multiple posts or threads

### 2. Be Honest

This community depends on accurate, reliable information.

- Do not post false gas prices, fake deals, or misleading earnings data
- Do not impersonate other users, admins, or companies (including Uber, Lyft, etc.)
- Do not spread misinformation about gig platforms, laws, or regulations
- If you are uncertain about something, say so

### 3. Stay On Topic

Posts and comments should be relevant to the sub-forum they are posted in.

- **c/Canada** — national topics: federal regulations, cross-province tips, industry news
- **c/[Zone]** — local topics: area-specific tips, local gas prices, zone events, regional deals
- Off-topic posts may be moved to the appropriate sub-forum by an Admin

### 4. No Spam or Self-Promotion

- Do not repeatedly post the same content
- Do not post unsolicited advertisements, affiliate links, or referral codes without Admin approval
- Do not use the community to recruit users to competing platforms or services
- Sponsored content is permitted only with Admin approval and must be clearly labelled

### 5. Protect Privacy

- Do not post personal information about other people (names, addresses, phone numbers, photos) without their explicit consent
- Do not post screenshots of private conversations without the other party's consent
- Do not attempt to identify anonymous or pseudonymous users
- Respect the privacy of passengers, pedestrians, or anyone who is not a member of this community

### 6. Keep It Legal

- Do not post content that is illegal under Canadian law, including the *Criminal Code of Canada*, the *Canadian Human Rights Act*, or applicable provincial law
- Do not post or link to pirated content, illegal software, or illicit goods or services
- Do not facilitate or encourage illegal activity related to gig work (e.g., tax fraud, unlicensed vehicle operation, insurance fraud)
- Do not post content that is defamatory

### 7. No Graphic or Adult Content

- No sexually explicit material
- No graphic violence
- No content that would be distressing or harmful to a general audience

## Gas Prices and Crowd-Sourced Data

Gas price submissions are relied upon by other drivers. When submitting prices:

- Submit only prices you have personally verified
- Submit the correct fuel type and station
- Do not submit false or manipulated prices — this is a violation of these guidelines and may constitute fraud

DriveTrack may remove inaccurate price submissions without notice.

## Moderation

### Who Moderates

- **DriveTrack administrators** have global moderation authority across all sub-forums
- **Zone Admins** moderate content within their assigned zone sub-forums
- Zone Admins are community members, not DriveTrack employees or agents

### What Moderators Can Do

- Remove posts or comments that violate these guidelines
- Issue warnings to users
- Temporarily suspend a user's posting privileges
- Permanently ban users for serious or repeated violations
- Pin or feature notable community posts

### What Moderators Cannot Do

- Access your private account data or financial records
- Contact you outside the App on behalf of DriveTrack
- Offer official legal or financial advice on DriveTrack's behalf

### Appealing a Moderation Decision

If you believe your content was removed unfairly, contact us at [CONTACT EMAIL] with the subject line "Moderation Appeal." Include the post in question and the reason you believe it should be reinstated. We will review appeals within 5 business days. Moderation decisions are final after appeal review.

---

## Reporting Content

If you see content that violates these guidelines, use the in-app "Report" button on any post or comment. Reports are reviewed by admins. We do not reveal the identity of the person who filed a report.

Do not use the report function as a tool to silence users you disagree with — false reports are themselves a violation of these guidelines.

---

## Consequences

Violations are handled progressively based on severity and history:

| Level | Example Violations | Consequence |
|---|---|---|
| Minor | Off-topic post, accidental spam | Content removed; no account action |
| Moderate | Repeated spam, mild harassment, false data | Warning + content removal |
| Serious | Targeted harassment, doxxing, hate speech | Temporary suspension (1–30 days) |
| Severe | Illegal content, repeat serious violations | Permanent ban; possible law enforcement referral |

DriveTrack reserves the right to skip progressive steps and issue an immediate permanent ban for severe violations.
